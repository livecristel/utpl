Home Automation

HOME/OFFICE AUTOMATION

Home Automation is an Electronic Automation System for a home. Home Automation allowing full control of your home from anywhere, anytime.

  • At based and IOT Enabled
  • Intelligent Routers
  • Hotspots and 4G Dongles
  • Touch Panel intelligent Home Assists
  • Sensors, Switches and Controllers

TOUCH SWITCHES

Simply touch the buttons to operate them like a regular switch which work without the internet, as switches should. Help to adjust the lights according to your requirements.

RGB CONTROLLER

The RGB LED Controllers allows you to take full control of your lighting and take it to the next level. Take advantage of countless combinations of colors and lighting effects, from presets to full customization.

FAN CONTROLLER

An intelligent control for your Fans. Compatible with a variety of fans. Controllable with your phone or voice assistant. Speed control for ease of use. UTPL's Delight fan controllers are guaranteed to make your life a heavens.

REMOTE POWER SWITCH

The Remote Switch allows you to control any number of appliances like AC, TV etc from anywhere. You can control the remote switch from your phone or voice assistants like Alexa & Google Assistant.

GANG SWITCH

Configured for Bedroom, Hall, Kitchen, Study rooms/offices.

PLUG & USB WALL CHARGER

Plug is compatible for vast variety of appliances such as AC, televisions, electric cookers, common area lighting, street lights, wet grinders, water coolers, air coolers and more. In-built countdown timer function for charging Mobiles and Laptops. Compatible with Alexa & Google Assistant.

DIMMER

Delight Dimmers are a convenience feature that offers additional control over the lighting in your living spaces by allowing you to adjust the brightness of the LED lights in a room via voice control (Alexa and Google). UTPL's Delight Home app, or a smart switch.

HOME/OFFICE AUTOMATION

The switchboard module fits behind your existing switchboard panel, without altering your electrical wiring or the interior dector. Retrofit Automation system can be accessed from multiple devices.

CURTAIN CONTROLLER

Automation control of your motorized curtains. Control your curtains from the comfort of your bed using UTPL's Delight Automation Switches & Controllers, touch panel switches, Delight Home app, IR Remotes or voice control through Alexa/ Google.

PIR SENSOR

Acting as virtual security guard in your house. Automatically turning on/off house lights when any person passes. These devices can greatly improve energy savings.
